### Stadt der Magie, Harmonie und Natur(?)

Luxivia, The Townforest under the Nightsky, lies far away from Aestival on the edge of the Brotherhood snowy, cold Thundra.

Mt.Aurora is the place Luxivians call home, because that's where their little Hometown is, on the bottom of the mountain.

  

Das besondere an Luxivia ist ihre enge Verbindung zur Natur. Die Stadt besteht aus Bauten, die uns Menschen bekannt sind, die überwuchert sind mit der Schöhnheit des Waldes

  

Because I was just thinking on a  concept of the City overall function and structure.

I imagine that the City has three "Rulers". Each of them represent one ethical group, a section of the city:

- The Druid, Priest of Aphyds

- The... uh... Spark (I couldnt Think of a good name yet, heha), Priest of Abel

- The Lightweaver, Priest of (Insert fitting God here).

  

Divided by three but one cannot exist without the other.

  

(It gets very metaphorical here)

The people that serve the Druid, represent life. Everything that is essential for every living thing. So, basically, they provide food. Maybe healers?

  

The people who serve under Priest of Abel represent the element of fire. Which stands for progress, infinite potential and change. This is the section where scientist and business man do their job. Life flourishes here.

  

And then there is the section of the Lightweaver. This section stands for harmony, order and chaos. This could be the central place for bureacracy.
