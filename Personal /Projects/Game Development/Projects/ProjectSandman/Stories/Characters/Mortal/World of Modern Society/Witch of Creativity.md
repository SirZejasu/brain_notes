# Steckbrief 

## About
Represents the problem me and some others with ADHD / ADS have irl. 
She was stuck between hobbies and Interests. She was barely good at anything but "tasted" everything.
After the outbreak she had some form of ability that helped her "multiply" her personality and help her focus on specific creative hobbies like Music, Writing, Drawing, Invention by connecting them.
Creativity comes in many forms and she represents one of these forms.

Scene-Idea: As a [[Witch-Beasts|Witch]] she takes a drug (weed?) that strengthens her ability where it get out control. It does not strengthen her creativity but she is able to see through the dream to point where her mind is bein catapulted into the [[Wald der Träume|Forest]]. 
Note to self: Take you own little experience with weed from Duinrell as a reference.
One of her awakening states let her slip through reality.
Maybe: She is able to see through time and see different interpretations of the same dream.
